Patrícia Rebello Teles


BSc in Mathematics at Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro (1991) and MSc in Physics at Physics Institut of São Paulo University (2006) researching Nuclear Physics applied to Medicine on the subject Proton Radiation Therapy. My advisor was Prof. Dr. Mahir Saleh Hussein from Mathematical Physics Department.
After that I continued at Mathematical Physics Department advisored by Prof. Dr. Oscar Pinto Éboli and on February 2008 I have begun to work on my PhD thesis at Universidade Federal do ABC.
We will focus on High Energy Particle Physics Phenomenology, searching new physics beyond Standard Model, which we hope will arise from Large Hadron Collider experiments working at CERN.
My advisor in this project is Prof. Dr. José Kenichi Mizukoshi.
